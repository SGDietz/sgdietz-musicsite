Frequently Asked Questions

Who is SG Dietz?

SG Dietz (Scott Gregory Dietz) is an independent recording artist from Bel Air, Maryland, now based between Nashville, Tennessee and Baltimore, Maryland. He started his music career in middle age and writes, composes, and arranges every song himself.

What are the Mismatched Plaids?

The Mismatched Plaids are the music — every major genre humans have ever made, coming from one artist. Country, hip-hop, metal, pop, dance, mariachi, blues, rock, comedy. It's not a style; it's a refusal to pick one. No two tracks sound alike, and nobody else takes it this far.

Does SG Dietz use AI in his music?

Everything in the current SG Dietz catalog is 100% human — every lyric, melody, and arrangement is written and created by SG Dietz himself. Going forward, he'll be experimenting with AI as part of the creative process, but the released songs you hear today (Fiesta Cancion, Bounce, 6 Foot Dick, Destruction Guitars, Mysterioso) come straight from a human mind and a human life.

Where can I listen to SG Dietz?

Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music, YouTube, Pandora, Deezer, and Amazon Music. Direct links are above.

What songs has SG Dietz released?

Fiesta Cancion (Spanglish-mariachi), Bounce (pop), 6 Foot Dick (country comedy), Destruction Guitars (acid rock), and Mysterioso (hip-hop instrumental).
