Who is SG Dietz?

An UNBRIDLED MIND with EXPLOSIVE CREATIVE ENERGY.

Background

Scott Gregory Dietz grew up in Bel Air, Maryland — about thirty minutes north of Baltimore, where his parents were born and raised.

Character

An undisciplined child that was impossible to control, evolved into an adult with an unbridled mind and explosive creative energy.

Work

Over decades I built a 12-person landscaping company and pursued various artistic ventures including music and comedy. I am a Master Stone Mason and Fine Artist — capable of design and construction work globally.

Now

Based between Nashville, Tennessee and Baltimore, Maryland — actively building relationships, music, comedy, and stone artistry.

Collaboration

I'm looking to work with established or emerging artists. Remember Old Town Road? Billy Ray Cyrus + Lil Nas X. Killer Track + Name Brand = SMASH HIT. If that sparks something for you, get in touch.
